About

Roboticist & Technopreneur

Mr. Shail Jadav, currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Gandhinagar (IIT Gandhinagar), focuses on robotics, control theory, and biomedical engineering. He obtained his B.E. in Biomedical Engineering from Gujarat Technological University in 2017. Following his undergraduate studies, he was part of the research staff at IIT Gandhinagar from 2017 to 2018, before embarking on his doctoral studies in 2018.

  • Email: shail-dot-jadav-at-iitgn-dot-ac-in
  • Website: www.shailjadav.github.io
  • Office: 305 Research Park, IIT Gandhinagar, India
  • City: Ahmedabad, India

Shail Jadav serves as the dedicated founder and CEO of Tesseract Robotics & Automation Private Limited, focusing on enhancing robotics in India through accessible motion control solutions. His company is committed to developing industrial-grade motion controllers, which have been implemented in various robots. With a deep understanding of motion control, Shail is a reliable resource for those seeking guidance or information in this specialized field.
